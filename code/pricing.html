Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Hybrid RAG?

Hybrid RAG combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation with private document indexing. Your data stays secure, AI stays smart, and you get grounded answers with source evidence—all in one workspace.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. For annual plans, you'll receive a prorated credit if you downgrade.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. All your documents are encrypted end-to-end. We never train on your data, and Enterprise customers can deploy on-premises for complete control.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! Students, educators, and academic institutions receive special pricing. Contact us to learn more about our academic program.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. Your billing information is secured with Stripe.
