Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is "Hybrid RAG"?

expand_more

Hybrid RAG combines local vector databases for sensitive data with cloud-based LLMs for general reasoning, ensuring your most private information never leaves your infrastructure while maintaining state-of-the-art performance.

Can I switch plans anytime?

expand_more

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings.

Do you offer educational discounts?

expand_more

Absolutely. Contact our sales team with your .edu email for special academic pricing.
